Our Services

Professional pavement solutions for residential and commercial properties

Sealcoating

Sealcoating is the most cost-effective way to extend the life of your asphalt pavement. Our professional sealcoating service creates a protective barrier against:

  • UV rays and oxidation damage
  • Water penetration and freeze-thaw damage
  • Oil and chemical spills
  • General wear and tear

Regular sealcoating every 2-3 years can double the life of your driveway or parking lot, saving you thousands in replacement costs.

Hot Rubber Crack Repairs

Cracks in your pavement are more than just an eyesore - they're entry points for water that can cause serious structural damage. Our hot rubber crack filling service:

  • Uses commercial-grade hot-applied rubberized sealant
  • Remains flexible through temperature changes
  • Prevents water from reaching the base
  • Stops cracks from spreading

Don't wait until small cracks become major repairs. Early crack filling is the key to preventing costly damage.

Hot Asphalt Patch Repair

For damaged areas, potholes, and deteriorated sections, our hot asphalt patch repair provides a permanent solution. We offer:

  • Pothole repair for driveways and parking lots
  • Edge repair along curbs and borders
  • Depression and low-spot correction
  • Utility cut repairs

Our hot asphalt patches are compacted to match the surrounding pavement, creating a seamless, long-lasting repair.
