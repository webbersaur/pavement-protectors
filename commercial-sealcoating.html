Commercial Sealcoating

Parking lot sealcoating, line striping, and asphalt maintenance for Connecticut businesses

Protect Your Commercial Pavement

Your parking lot is the first thing customers see and one of your largest property assets. Pavement Protectors helps Connecticut businesses keep their lots safe, professional, and protected with commercial-grade sealcoating and pavement maintenance.

Regular sealcoating shields your asphalt from UV damage, water penetration, oil, and freeze-thaw cycles — extending pavement life and helping you avoid costly resurfacing. We work around your business hours to keep disruption to a minimum.

  • Commercial parking lot sealcoating
  • Line striping, ADA stalls, fire lanes & arrows
  • Hot rubber crack filling & asphalt patch repair
  • Scheduled maintenance programs

Parking Lot Line Striping

Crisp, compliant pavement markings improve traffic flow, maximize parking capacity, and keep your property safe and ADA-compliant. We restripe as part of a sealcoating project or as a standalone service.

  • Standard, compact & ADA-accessible stalls
  • Fire lanes, loading zones & crosswalks
  • Directional arrows, stop bars & stencils
  • Bright, durable traffic-grade paint
